How it works

A consent layer for everyday group photos.

WhoTook starts with a protected version of the photo, then gives matched people a clear choice before their face appears normally.

01

Capture safely

The photographer takes a photo and WhoTook creates a protected preview first.

02

Ask for consent

Matched people receive a request and can review the protected preview.

03

Approve or protect

The gallery shows the approved original or a protected result for denied faces.
